# Translating Model Outputs into a Stakeholder-Ready Decision Memo

## Scenario

All analyses for the subscription tier launch have been completed. Pre-computed results summarizing key findings across choice modeling, propensity analysis, positioning, and marketing mix are provided in the notebook. Your goal is to synthesize these into a decision-ready memo that a non-technical stakeholder can act on — with clear recommendations, supporting evidence, and explicit limitations.

## Your task

Using the starter notebook provided in the workspace, produce:

1. A one-page structured decision memo with all five decisions populated
2. A supporting evidence chart (bundle scores or MMM channel coefficients)

## Data

All results are pre-computed and provided in the notebook. No additional data files are required.

## Requirements

Your memo must use the provided template headings and explicitly state decisions for:
1. **Bundle** — name the recommended bundle with a score reference
2. **Price / WTP** — state a price point and reference at least one WTP value
3. **Offer** — state whether to scale, refine, or hold the offer, referencing IPW ATE vs naive ATE
4. **Positioning / Messaging** — state a positioning angle referencing the MDS closest substitute pair and whitespace bundle
5. **Budget** — name channels to prioritize or deprioritize, referencing MMM coefficients

Additional requirements:
- The sentence *"MMM results are correlational (directional), not causal."* must appear verbatim in the budget section
- Assumptions & Limitations section must include at least four bullet points covering the choice model, WTP, IPW, and MMM
- Next Steps section must include at least one concrete validation action

## Deliverable

Your completed notebook with the memo markdown fully filled in, all five decisions populated with specific numeric evidence, and at least one supporting chart produced from the pre-computed results.
